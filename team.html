About us

Network for Quality, Productivity, and Competitiveness – Nepal (NQPCN) is a Network of Entrepreneurs, Managers, Academicians, and Consultants of Nepal was established in 2004 to enhance competitiveness through sharing, learning and applying globally developed quality and productivity tools and techniques for social and economic development and the nation.

NQPCN is a not-for-profit organization solely run from membership fees and voluntary contributions.
